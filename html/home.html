Electric Hole In The Wall

Our inspiration to create this game came from popular comedy Japanese TV Show "Hole In the Wall." The goal of the game is to use charge clumps to "move" a charged object to a hole (mirroring ths show, in which participants have to contort their bodies to fit an outline within a short period of time). The primary physics principle used is Coulomb's law.


Settings

You will have the option to choose between three modes of difficulty: EASY (relatively simple object with no barriers), MEDIUM (relativelt simple object with barriers randomly scattered), and HARD ( complex shapes and barriers). In addition to difficulty levels, there will be the option to choose between a ZEN and TIMED mode, with the former featuring an infinite number of levels and no time limit, and the latter having a finite number of levels to be completed in a set time limit.
