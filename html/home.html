Electric Hole In The Wall

Our inspiration to create this game came from popular comedy Japanese TV Show "Hole In the Wall," in which the goal of the game is to use charged clumps to move a charged object to a hole (mirroring the show, in which participants have to contort their bodies to fit an outline within a short period of time). The primary physics principle used is Coulomb's law.

Settings

We can also add random charge distributions to the objects to narrow down the ways to achieve the objective.

You will also have the option to adjust the TIMED mode to SLOW, NORMAL, and QUICK to increase or decrease difficulty.
